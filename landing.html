A college with a difference!!

Fr. CRIT. has, within a short span of time, established itself as a leading engineering college in Mumbai University. Though its reputation rests mainly on the high quality, value-based technical education that it imparts, it has to its credit a verdant, well-maintained Campus and extensive facilities. Its location in the vicinity of the holy places of various religious denominations underscores its secular credentials and its philosophy of "Vasudhaiva Kuttumbakam". The college prides on being one of few that has accreditation for all five branches. Fr. C. Rodrigues Institute of Technology
